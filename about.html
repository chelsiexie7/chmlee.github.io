尽人事，听天命。
Do what you love, and connect the dots.

你好呀，我是阿驰，一个喜欢研究咨询心理学的年轻人。
Hi, I am Chi, a youth loves studying counselling psychology.

我六年前想读哲学专业，五年前开始寻找"什么是好的教育"，一年前为了探索心理学与舞蹈相结合的可能，选择了认知神经科学方向的硕士项目。闲暇时间，我到传媒行业、社会创新行业和学术科研行业探索了一番。
Well, six years ago I loved philosophy; five years ago I looked for the definition of "good education"; one year ago I tried to combined psychology and dance together and choose Cognitive Neuroscience as my study track. In my spare time, I explored the fields of journalism, entrepreneurship and academic research.

当我从学前教育学系毕业的时候，我为我自己当年的困惑找到了答案："好的教育"应该是提供一个合适的平台——这个平台能够促进学生之间的相互学习、能够让学生获得有经验者/专业人士的指导，能够让学生看见自己的成长与成就。
When I graduated from the School of Early Childhood Ecuation in Shanghai, I found my answer to "how is the good education look like": providing an appropriate platform where students can learn from each other, be exposed to the guidance from experienced and excerpts, and visualise their accomplishments.

当我决定投身于咨询心理学时，我决定为我的同伴和我自己创造这样的有效学习机制。于是，2020年初，基于我的微信公众号阿驰附近，我创办了华人心理咨询师专业成长小组和豆瓣心理咨询专业成长小组。
As I made my mind to devoted myself into Counselling Psychology, I decided to offer myself and my peers such an effective platform. So, at the beginning of 2020, I founded a peer support group for Chinese novice counsellors around the world, which named CP Group.

截止至2020年5月1日，已有数十人表达了想要加入小组的意愿。小组成员会在小组中与同伴分享自己在专业学习中的Aha时刻，举行定期的学术和职业专题研讨，并为处于困境的伙伴提供支持。如果你也是心理咨询和助人行业的从业者，我期待你的加入。
By May 1, 2020, tens of students showed their interests in joinin this group. As a group member, he/she shares their Aha moments in their studying process, launches panel discussions about academic/professional life, and supports the peers who are in struggle. Let's studying counselling psychology with credible peers.

很高兴能跟现在的一切相遇:）
I am glad that I finally connect my dots today:)
